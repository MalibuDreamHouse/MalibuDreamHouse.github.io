Rules

These are some rules for everyone to follow to make sure all of the events go smoothly. Please follow them or get kicked out.

THE #1 RULE
DON'T ASK THE PENNIES

Ask people that have been at the house before or anyone else that's around. It sucks to throw a party and answer literally 200 questions in a day. Instead invite them to a conversation, or bring them a treat

Only Smoke In The Circle Of Shame

You can smoke green stuff anywhere, but keep cigarettes confined to the circle of shame.

Do Not Use the Inside Restrooms

Use the compost toilets. That's what they're there for. The septic system can't handle as many people that come through. The Restrooms in the house are reserved for the Pennies and guests with permission.

Pee In the Bushes

This one's for the men. If you just need to pee, go in the bushes helps to scare away the deer and reduces the need to deal with the compost toilets.

Pack It In Pack It Out

Bring food, Bring coolers to keep food cool, bring all sorts of stuff. Just remember to TAKE IT HOME the Malibu Dream House does not need 20 permanent coolers, or 60 Hummus containers.

Keep The Doors Closed

Make sure every door and screen are always closed. The Pennies have cats and want to keep them. Remind people if you see them leaving doors open, but close them first it's everyone's responsibilities.

Quiet Time

Quiet Hours in the house starting ~4:30am. If you are still awake and happy, the garage and outdoor areas are available though the main sound system will be turned off. There's speakers for an iPod or laptop in the garage, and acoustic guitars are so lovely. Nobody in the house during quiet hours unless you are sleeping.
