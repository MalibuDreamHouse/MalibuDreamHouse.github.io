Harsh Rules

Harsh Rules are strictly enforced for the safety and joy of all our invited guests. Please police each other. We take them seriously, and so should you.

THE #1 RULE
DON'T ASK THE HOSTS

We have many regulars who will gladly help you, if the answer is not already on this website. It is too stressful on your hosts to answer 200+ questions in a single evening.

No Plus Ones

Our events are direct invitation only, which preserves the safety and friendly energy of our events. Your spouse is welcome. No randoms. No last minute requests.

Only Smoke In The Circle Of Shame

You can smoke green stuff or vapor anywhere outdoors, but cigarettes are strictly confined to the Circle of Shame. No chairs allowed in the Circle of Shame.

Contact Info on Every Parked Car

It is REQUIRED for every car parked on premise to have a notecard with your full name and number clearly displayed on the dash. If there are any issues, we must know who you are and how to reach you.

Close Doors for Cats

Doors must stay closed QUICKLY so our cats Midas, Zumee, and Mayebelle stay safe indoors. The only exception is the slider that leads to the Catio. Plenty of signs to remind you.

No Glass Anywhere/Closed Containers

This is a rule upgrade from "No Glass by the Pool". No glass anywhere! Pour all drinks directly into a sealed non-glass container such as a travel mug or sippy cup, to reduce spills. Bring Your Own Cup!

BYO Everything & Label All The Things

Our events are not catered. Bring everything you need to take care of yourself including food, drinks, ice, cooler, tools, tent, pillow, etc. Sharing is encouraged but not required. Take home your leftovers. Healthy food encouraged! Label All Your Things!

Clean Up After Yourselves and Others

"Cleaning as we go" is very central to our culture here. This means everyone! Here's a partial list of things to do during the party: Take out the trash. Straighten up the food table. Throw away empty bottles from the bar. Wipe up spills. Gather & wash used cups.

Use Outdoor Kitchen, not Indoor

The outdoor area near the jacuzzi is now an "almost fully functional" kitchen. When you arrive, take your drinks, food & coolers directly to this area NOT the indoor kitchen. Grill/eat/drink/wash dishes all in one area. More details here:

ZTFA

ZTFA means "Zero Tolerance for A--holes". Leave your drama at the door, don't get out of control, don't break any Harsh Rules. If there's a ZTFA issue, you will be asked to leave immediately and no longer welcome here. If anyone has a concern about a guest based on behavior at other events, please advise your hosts proactively.

Towel & Robe System

Towels & robes are available for your use. Ask a regular if you can't find them. Save water! Towel Fairies are volunteers (like you!) who put damp towels in the dryer, fold & return them. Otherwise, they'll be wet and smelly the next day, requiring many loads of laundry. You may hang your towel on a hook if you'll soak again later.

Compost Toilets

Indoor restrooms are OFF LIMITS. Our septic can't manage large crowds. There are two outdoor compost toilets: the blue "Turdis" or the white Compost House in front. Click here for helpful instructions.

Men Can Pee in the Field

Men: Rather than fill up our compost toilets quickly, you are welcome to go pee PRIVATELY along the fence/property line. Urine helps scare away the deer & coyotes.

Quiet Time

Quiet Hours are from 4 - 9am, which means all amplified sound is turned off. If you are still awake and happy, you can hang out in the garage or any outdoor areas, talking at normal volumes. Nobody in the house unless you're sleeping.

A Drum is Not A Table

We say this constantly, but at every party, someone puts their drink, keys, other items on one of our drums, our fish tank or a piece of art. PLEASE respect our nice things and the nice people who share them with you.

Clothing Optional Pool & Spa

Keep your clothes on, and your hands to yourself, in all public spaces. It's not that kind of event. You may remove your clothing for the pool & spa, but you can also wear a suit. Whatever is more comfortable.

Upstairs = Off Limits

Only the hosts and pre-approved guests are allowed upstairs. Nobody is allowed upstairs without the hosts approval.

Finding Us

We don't publish our street address. If you use GPS, enter "El Matador State Beach, Malibu, CA 90265" and look for the driveway directly across from it. Ours is the third/last house at the top of the driveway, blue tile roof and brightly lit at night.

If the gate is closed, onsite parking might be full. You can dial 002 at the gate to be buzzed in (if we answer). Otherwise park on PCH and then walk the path to get around the gate and up our driveway.

Please be respectful of the neighbors who share our driveway! Do not walk, park or linger anywhere near/on their property. Be quiet when passing by.
