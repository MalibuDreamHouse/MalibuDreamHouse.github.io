Parking

Follow the rules and make everyones life easier

  • Carpool Please

    The best way to park at the Malibu Dream House is to park with people. There's limited availability of spaces please come with people and carpool. There are a bit fewer than 30 parking spaces available at the house so we need to make sure we bring as many people as we can in as few cars as we can.

  • Notecard With Your Full Name and Number

    As you park ensure you leave a notecard in your window with your full name and cell phone number. This is so if anything happens someone needs you to move or anything we can contact you. No one wants to track the owner of a mystery car.

  • Listen To The Parking Czar

    At the larger events parking Czars will be recruited. Listen to them. If there isn't a Parking Czar helping you ensure you read the rest of the rules

    MAKE SURE YOU HAVE A NOTECARD IN YOUR CAR.

  • Go Long, Go Deep

    If you're staying a while go as deep as you can on the slopes and park there. Park on the hills. If you get stuck there will be help.

    You can only park in the flat spots if you're camping in your car.

    If you're not staying very long then try and use the easier access parking so we don't have to move people that have gone long and deep.

  • Follow The Signs

    Parking Czars will put out cones, and there should be well marked parking and tent markers. There are photo's below describing how to use them but use your common sense.
